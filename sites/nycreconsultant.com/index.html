About Us

Who We Are

NYC Real Estate Consultant provides independent, unbiased advisory services to real estate investors, developers, family offices, and institutions operating in the New York City market. Unlike brokers or developers who have transactional conflicts of interest, we operate on a pure fee-for-service model — our only incentive is to give you the most accurate, actionable analysis possible. Our principals have underwritten over $1 billion in NYC real estate transactions and bring deep expertise in multifamily, commercial, development, and regulatory environments.
